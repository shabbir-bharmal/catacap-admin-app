## Philanthropic Investing: Reimagining how capital creates change

- Medium: News article
- Audience: Donors/Investors
- Theme: Impact Investing
- Date: November 4, 2025

Description

There’s a paradox at the heart of modern philanthropy: a gap between mission and intent. Each year, billions of dollars flow into charitable vehicles such as foundations, trusts, and donor-advised funds (DAFs). Yet an estimated 95% of that money is parked in traditional public markets — stocks, mutual funds, and bonds — often invested in companies whose activities directly contradict the donor’s stated mission or values.

[Read full article](https://impactentrepreneur.com/philanthropic-investing/)

## How To Unlock Mission- Driven Investing With Your Donations

- Medium: News article
- Audience: Donors/Investors
- Theme: Impact Investing
- Date: September 11, 2025

Description

Billions of dollars intended for good are stuck in limbo, rather than flowing toward mission-driven investing. [Donor-advised funds](https://www.philanthropy.com/article/daf-assets-250-billion-and-counting?utm) (DAFs)—charitable vehicles where donors receive an immediate tax deduction but can decide later where the money goes—hold more than $250 billion in assets. Yet most of this capital sits in traditional stock and bond portfolios rather than supporting entrepreneurs who are tackling climate change, healthcare inequities, and other pressing social problems. This represents a massive missed opportunity.

## Sea Forward Joins CataCap to Make Ocean Impact Investing More Accessible

- Medium: Press release
- Audience: All
- Theme: Impact Investing
- Date: August 13, 2025

Description

Sea Forward is proud to announce a new opportunity for individuals to ‘donate to invest’ in ocean health, made possible through a launch on **CataCap**, a first-of-its-kind platform democratizing access to impact investing with philanthropic assets.

Individuals can now contribute as little as **$250** to support ocean health in high-impact areas with the potential to see returns to reinvest or grant out again. These donor investments, previously available only to major funders, can now be made into the [Global Fund for Coral Reefs](https://app.catacap.org/invest/CoralReefs) or across the broader [Sea Forward portfolio](https://app.catacap.org/invest/seaforward), which includes a [diverse set of investments](https://www.seaforwardfund.org/investments) into select ocean impact funds.

- Medium: Article on ImpactAlpha
- Audience: All
- Theme: Impact Investing
- Date: July 11, 2025

Description

What if our capital actually reflected the future we want?

For decades, capital in the social sector has been trapped in a binary: grants on one end, venture capital on the other. But that split has left too many visionary founders stranded in between – too early for VC, too catalytic for traditional philanthropy.

The solution may lie in a quietly growing model: \*philanthropic impact investing\*. It’s not new, but it’s finally gaining the visibility – and urgency – it deserves.

## Advancing Women’s Hormonal Health

- Medium: Video
- Audience: Investments
- Theme: Gender equity
- Date: May 1, 2025

Description

In this episode of Catalytic Capital, host Ken Kurtzig dives into the transformative world of Pandida Health, a company revolutionizing women’s healthcare by making birth control, reproductive care, and menopause treatment more accessible, affordable, and stigma free.

Today, Pandia is the highest-rated women’s online hormonal health clinic with 4.9/5 stars from >3K women, offering telemedicine and pharmacy services under one roof.

By addressing the $28B US birth control and menopause market, we are building “The Online Health Brand Women Trust” offering expert, evidence-based treatments, 90% of which is still untapped by telemedicine services.

Pandia Health currently operates at $2.5M ARR, and is forecast to grow to $2.3B ARR within 5 years. Revenue streams to date include: Online consultations with specialist doctors in hormonal care who write prescriptions to insured and uninsured women, delivery of birth control, emergency contraception and mHT to all 50 states.
