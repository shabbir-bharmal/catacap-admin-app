Write a complete “About Us / About CataCap” page for a website.

Use the structure of the original About CataCap page from https://www.catacap.org/about/ to generate engaging, professional, and SEO-friendly content in clear sections.

Include:

1. **Hero Intro** — A concise paragraph explaining what CataCap is and why it exists, emphasizing its mission to connect donors with impactful investments and democratize impact investing. 

2. **Core Mission & Vision** — A detailed mission statement about empowering individuals and organizations to transform philanthropic capital into purpose-driven investments that create measurable social and environmental outcomes.

3. **How It Works** — A clear step-by-step explanation of the donor journey:
   - Activating impact with a contribution via donor account
   - Joining and pooling capital with like-minded donors
   - Recommending impact investments or grants

4. **Values & Philosophy** — Short descriptions of values like inclusivity, lowering barriers to impact investing, fostering collaborative communities, supporting both donors and entrepreneurs equally, and innovation in philanthropy.

5. **Founders** — Two subsections with brief bios of the founders:
   - *Tim Freundlich* — his role, background in product design/business development, and commitment to social impact.
   - *Ken Kurtzig* — serial entrepreneur with experience building sustainability technology and mentoring mission-driven startups.

6. **Why Choose CataCap** — Key benefits for donors and investors, such as:
   - Lower minimums compared to traditional impact investing
   - Tax-deductible contributions
   - Collaborative investment community
   - Opportunity to reinvest returns for ongoing impact

7. **Call to Action** — Two short CTAs:
   - “Ready to get started? Create an account”
   - “Learn more about investments and impact opportunities”

Write in a friendly, inviting, and professional tone suitable for a nonprofit impact investing platform. Target length ~600–900 words.
